ABOUT SOMATIC EXPERIENCING

SE resolves symptoms of stress, shock, and trauma that accumulate in our bodies. When we are stuck in patterns of fight, flight, or freeze, SE helps us release, recover, and become more resilient. The large majority of people worldwide have traumatic experiences in their lifetime, which can have major impacts on their health and well-being.

SE™ Professional Training

The Somatic Experiencing® (SE) Professional Training is a three-year program designed to support a gradual and embodied learning process. The training is structured into three levels — Beginning, Intermediate, and Advanced — each completed over one year. Every level includes three 4-day modules, providing a total of 12 days of providing a total of 12 days of live interactive training sessions per level.
